Design Your Learning Partner

Set six design choices for an AI Learning Partner and watch how each one aligns with the wants and needs of the learners who use it, the teachers accountable for it, and the administrators who fund and govern it.

There is no perfect setting. Every choice helps someone and costs someone else.

To Design Your Learning Partner:

  1. Pick one of the three options for each design choice.
  2. Observe how your choices affect the three stakeholder groups.
